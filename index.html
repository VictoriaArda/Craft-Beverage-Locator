ABOUT

We know how large disrtibutors are run, we worked for them. That’s why we’re hell-bent on conducting ourselves differently. Liquid Runner is boutique by design. We’re dedicated to the client experience, the process and consistent communication through every step. We’ve never been interested in taking on as many brands as possible. It’s our mission to embrace the right suppliers so that we can focus on every detail and provide a great experience.

When we hit capacity, we’re at capacity. We don’t overload ourselves just to chase after the almighty dollar. We won’t sacrifice the client experience and the quality of our work to bring in more suppliers. It’s ultimately not what’s best for you or for us. The difference between working with Liquid Runner or someone else? No lifetime contracts ever! We’ll push ourselves to oblivion for your deadline, your challenges and for world-class quality of work.
